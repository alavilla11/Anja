Anja Lavilla, 2/20/24 Autobiography

ABOUT

Born and raised in the Philippines, a student in MIT International School.


-

I have been studying in MITIS for 12 years now. I started off as a kindergardener, the first ever kindergardener of MIT, now a grade 11 student. I basically have spent more time in school than my home.

Every year seems to get more challenging and challenging. As expected the higher grade you are the harder the subjects, but I didnt realize the longer I am in school the longer life gets difficult too. I have learned so much and met so much people. I have made so much friends, some left, some stayed. As I grow up in this school, I slowly got to know more about myself, my goals, and my priorities.

Despite the challenges, I have learned to move on and accept reality. I know that I'm not alone and I'm not the only one experiencing these challenges. I am grateful for all the good and bad days in school. To the kidergarden me, I hope I've made her proud.
